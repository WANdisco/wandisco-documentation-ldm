Contacting Support#

The best way for you to reach our 24x7 Support team is to raise a case on the WANdisco Support page by clicking on the Get Support link. However if your issue is urgent, call us using one of the following phone numbers.

You will need to reference your customer PIN when first contacting us over the phone. For information on how to retrieve your PIN, take a look at this Knowledge Base article.
